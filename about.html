My Story

I graduated from Duquesne University in 1987 and have built my career in financial services ever since. As a financial advisor with Medallion Wealth Management Inc., I provide objective financial guidance and investment advice to individual and corporate clients.

Since becoming a Certified Divorce Financial Analyst速 in 2008, I have specialized in helping clients, couples, and attorneys navigate the complex financial landscape of divorce. My focus is on clarifying complex financial issues and modeling the long-term implications of various settlement options.

I believe that understanding the full financial picture is essential during divorce proceedings. My goal is to provide the clarity and analysis needed to make informed decisions that will impact your financial future for years to come.
