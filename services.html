Social Security Offset Calculations

Under Pennsylvania law, a civil service employee may exclude from equitable distribution that portion of pension estimated to be received in lieu of Social Security.

I provide hypothetical Social Security offset calculations for cases involving employees who do not contribute to Social Security.

QDRO Preparation & Like-Orders

Qualified Domestic Relations Orders for retirement plan division

What is a QDRO?

A Qualified Domestic Relations Order (QDRO) is a legal order that divides retirement plan benefits between divorcing spouses. It is required to properly divide 401(k)s, pensions, and other qualified retirement plans.

Without a properly drafted QDRO, you may not receive your entitled share of retirement benefits, even if your divorce decree awards them to you.

Services Include

  • QDRO drafting for 401(k), 403(b), and pension plans
  • Like-orders for government and military plans
  • Plan administrator coordination
  • Review and revision of existing QDROs

Marital Tracing & Equalizations

Distinguishing marital from non-marital property

Not all assets acquired during a marriage are considered marital property. Inheritances, gifts, and pre-marital assets may be excluded from equitable distribution under certain circumstances.

I help trace the origin and growth of assets to properly identify and value both marital and non-marital components.

Services Include
